Our Story

Born from a passion for fine clothing

New Akshat Fashion was founded in 2012 by Akshat Rajgor with a single vision — to bring premium quality clothing to the families of Bhujpur and Kutch at honest prices. What began as a modest showroom on Main Road has grown into the region's most respected fashion destination.

Rooted in the rich textile heritage of Gujarat, we curate every piece with intention. From handwoven Banarasi silks and Bandhani prints to contemporary menswear and kids' fashion, our collection reflects the full spectrum of Indian family dressing — traditional, festive, casual, and everything in between.

We believe that great clothing should not be a luxury reserved for a few. At New Akshat Fashion, premium quality meets accessible pricing — because every family deserves to look and feel extraordinary.

Uncompromising Fabric Standards

Every textile in our showroom is handpicked and quality-checked. We stock only fabrics that meet our strict standards for feel, durability, and finish — from pure cottons to premium silks.

02 — VARIETY

Something for Every Family Member

Men's sherwanis and suitings, women's sarees and kurtis, kids' ethnic and casual wear — our showroom is a complete family fashion destination under one roof in Bhujpur.
